Luca Marcelli hails from a small town in Italy, where coffee culture is deeply rooted in daily life. With over 15 years of experience in the coffee industry, Luca has mastered the art of espresso. He trained at prestigious coffee academies in Milan and has worked in several renowned cafes across Europe. Known for his precision and passion, Luca’s signature drinks often feature innovative twists on classic Italian coffee recipes. He believes in sourcing the finest beans and emphasizes sustainability in his coffee-making practices.

Luca Marcelli

Diego Sanchez grew up in the vibrant coffee regions of Colombia. His deep connection to coffee started at a young age, as his family owned a coffee plantation. Diego's expertise spans from bean cultivation to the final brew. After studying at a leading culinary institute in Bogotá, he traveled the world to explore various coffee cultures. Diego is renowned for his ability to bring out unique flavors through experimental brewing methods. He often hosts coffee-tasting events and workshops, sharing his vast knowledge and passion with others.

Diego Sanchez

Ethan Caldwell, an American barista and coffee innovator, has been a prominent figure in the third-wave coffee movement. Based in Portland, Oregon, Ethan has been a barista for over a decade. He’s known for his artistic latte art and creative specialty drinks, often incorporating local ingredients. Ethan is a certified Q grader, meaning he is an expert in coffee quality assessment. His dedication to the craft extends to mentoring aspiring baristas and participating in international coffee competitions.
